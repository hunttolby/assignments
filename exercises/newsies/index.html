Many Coders found DEAD after coffee overdose!

In downtown Salt Lake City, the bootcamp web developer school known as V School, had a terrible accident last Wednesday. Over 14 students were found dead!

V School students wake up early and work extremely hard to perfect their code. They need the extra energy they get from coffee! Most V School students drink 837 cups of coffee every day.

Extreme amounts of coffee like this, have now lead to over 14 students dying from coffee overdosage. V School released a public statement, "We do not take responsibility for these deaths as they made their own coffee."
